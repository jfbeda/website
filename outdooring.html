Outdooring

I have a keen interest in hiking/canoeing/mountaineering/biking/skiing/running/climbing and all sorts of things outside. In Canada I primarily enjoy admiring the lovely trees and lakes of Algonquin and the Kawartha Highlands. In the UK, where fewer trees, lakes, and canoes are to be found, I enjoy climbing all of the various grassy, rocky, and snowy lumps around whenever I can.
